    What is KRISHISAHAY

    • KRISHISAHAY (Knowledge Repository & Information System for Hindustani Agriculture: Support And Help for Yields) is a website particularly for the Indian Farmers to provide all information in one portal.

    What does KRISHISAHAY mean?

    • KRISHISAHAY - Knowledge Repository & Information System for Hindustani Agriculture: Support And Help for Yields
    • Krishi Sahay" (कृषि सहाय) in Hindi translates to "Agricultural Aid" or "Farmers' Help" in English. Same as a mirror our website also reflects out all the info that's out there without misleading.

    Reasons to Use KRISHISAHAY

    • A farmer will be able to get all relevant information on specific subjects around his state.
    • One Stop Solution for Farmers with most of the required Info available at one place.
    • Consist of features like weather report, government schemes, seed price etc.
